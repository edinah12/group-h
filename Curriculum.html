    Course Details

    Mathematics covers topics such as algebra, geometry, and calculus. Students will develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

    Science includes subjects like physics, chemistry, and biology. The focus is on experimentation and understanding the natural world.

    History explores key events and figures that shaped the world, from ancient civilizations to modern-day society.

    Literature involves studying novels, poetry, and plays. Students will analyze themes, characters, and the literary styles of various authors.



    Our intimate class sizes, with an impressive 1:45 teacher-to-student ratio, ensure personalized attention, close monitoring, and a learning environment that supports each student’s unique journey.
