About Our School

St. Mary’s Boarding Secondary School Kitende (SMASK) was established in the year 2001 in Wakiso district by Dr. Lawrence Mulindwa, with an aim to provide world class education and promote sports in Uganda. We have registered success in both the education and sports fields, breaking and making numerous records and milestones in the process. The school is registered with the ministry of education and sports with a centre number 1224 and offers both science and arts subjects for the Advanced level and equally avails the government curriculum of education for O'level. It's boys and girls mixed boarding school located in wakiso district
